Who I am

Danh was previously a physical therapist assistant who aspired to become a full-stack software developer. As a PTA, he prioritized efficiency while maintaining effectiveness of treatment. Due to the nature of medical field, there is a high emphasis on teamwork in order to provide the best care necessary for his patients. He finished full stack bootcamp at DigitalCraft in 09/22, which strives to create innovated and competitive software developers. Danh considered himself a forever student who is eager to build on his software developer foundation.
